About

Drop The Disco Ball, established in 2010.

  • 2010

    Garage brand

    Drop The Disco Ball began as a screenprinting and design company in my mom's gargage. Lots of lessons learned – most importantly that I don't like Excel.

  • 2012-2017

    DTDB goes dormant

    Drop The Disco Ball goes off the airwaves. Allyssa becomes an in-house designer and eventually a web developer at a well-known start-up. Constantly working to hone her craft.

  • October 2017

    Back in action

    After doing the corporate gig and spending nearly 4 years in the startup world I decide it's time to bring back Drop The Disco Ball. I am ready to use everything I've learned in the last 5 years.

  • Present

    Rebirth

    Drop The Disco Ball aims to be a freelance option for companies, small businesses and individuals alike. No longer just a garage brand – we've moved into the house and we're here to help with your design and marketing efforts. I still don't like Excel.
