I am HubSpot Design Certified

I'm a HubSpot developer available for freelance or contract work. I have been working with HubSpot for three years and I am HubSpot design and marketing certified. Not only can I create HubSpot templates I can also read and understand existing code to find and fix bugs. I have also been working with HubSpot CRM for the last 18 months. I am able to create and implement workflows, create and edit existing forms, create/track email campaigns, and tailor and customize lead scoring to fit your metrics.

CAPABILITIES

HubSpot CMS

I can create landing page, site page and email templates within the HubSpot design manager. I can also create original CSS files to fit your brand. I will manage url redirects, keep your site map up-to-date, ensure Google Analytics is enabled and working and check for deadlinks and pages. Additionally, I can also ensure everything is up-to-date, submitted and crawled in the Google Search Console.

Workflows and Metrics

I can ensure all HubSpot tracking is properly implemented and working. I can tie in lead flows, forms and third-party applications into your HubSpot workflows. I can also ensure all pages are being considered (or not considered) into your HubSpot lead scoring. I use HubSpot campaigns and their tracking url builder to ensure everything is being tracked correctly and in the right spots. I can also create custom event using the HubSpot events tool and turn them into tailored reports for your team.

HubSpot CRM

I can ensure all leads from the HubSpot marketing side are connected to the CRM platform. I can craft dashboards using the filters your sales team needs. If you need certain attributes or information acquired from lead forms I can create them and tie them into the contact view on the CRM side. I can also manage the documents panel to ensure your team is working with the latest content and collateral.
